About the Academy

Established in 2020, Marg Darshan Sports Academy has nurtured over 100+ cricketers with a passion for the game and a commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted grassroots cricket academy — shaping confident, skilled, and disciplined athletes who represent the best of the sport.

Our Mission

Provide world-class coaching, sports science integration, and competitive match exposure under one roof. We believe every child deserves access to professional training, regardless of background.

Training Philosophy

We follow a structured, progressive curriculum blending classical techniques with modern analytics. Every player gets a personalized development plan tracked through video analysis, fitness benchmarks, and regular assessments.

Why Choose Us

  • ICC Level-2 certified coaching staff
  • professional net practice facility
  • Dedicated fitness and strength conditioning
  • Regular inter-academy matches & tournaments
  • Video analysis with frame-by-frame feedback
  • Specialized batting, bowling & fielding modules
  • Mental conditioning & sports psychology sessions
  • Affordable monthly & quarterly fee plans

Safety & Code of Conduct

Your child's safety and well-being is our top priority.

Safety Guidelines

  • Mandatory helmets and protective gear during practice and matches
  • First-aid kit and trained personnel present at all sessions
  • Regular equipment inspection and maintenance schedule
  • Hydration breaks every 30 minutes during summer sessions
  • CCTV monitoring across the facility for security

Code of Conduct

  • Respect coaches, fellow students, and opponents at all times
  • Punctuality — arrive 10 minutes before session time
  • No use of abusive language or aggressive behavior
  • Follow the spirit of the game — fair play above all
  • Parents/guardians are welcome to observe but not interfere during sessions
  • Any form of bullying or discrimination will result in immediate action
